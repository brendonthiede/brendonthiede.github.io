The LoveLocal 5K is in support of the Compassion Ministry at Riverview Church. Race will be at Potter Park, going east on the Rivertrail, at 7:00pm on Friday, June 12th, 2015.

And here's my challenge... For every runner that places ahead of me I will donate $20 to the Compassion Ministry. If you want more details or want to persuade me with a different "side wager" you can add a comment.

Other details: chip timing by Chuck Block, age group awards, several gift cards will be awarded. The gift cards that I know of are $20 gift cards to various local places that will be awarded to age group winners as well as $100 gift certificates to Capital Prime for one male and one female (not sure if it's a drawing or for overall winners).

Hope to see you there!
