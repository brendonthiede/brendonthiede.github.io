Information Distribution

  • Face to face/Phone
  • Email
  • SharePoint
  • Slack

Face to face/Phone

  • Most natural
  • Most emotive
  • Transient

Email

  • Most distributable
  • Semi-permanent
  • Searchable
  • Fragile continuity
  • Medium frequency/Medium size

SharePoint

  • Most permanent
  • Versioned
  • Most searchable
  • Collaboration tools
  • Low frequency/High size

Slack

  • Loosely structured
  • Strict arrival continuity
  • Many integrations
  • Searchable
  • High frequency/Low size
