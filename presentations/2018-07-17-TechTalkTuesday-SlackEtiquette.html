Information Distribution

  • Face to face/Phone
  • Email
  • SharePoint
  • Slack

Face to face/Phone

  • Most natural
  • Most emotive
  • Transient

Email

  • Most distributable
  • Semi-permanent
  • Searchable
  • Fragile continuity
  • Medium frequency/Medium size

SharePoint

  • Most permanent
  • Versioned
  • Most searchable
  • Collaboration tools
  • Low frequency/High size

Slack

  • Loosely structured
  • Strict arrival continuity
  • Many integrations
  • Searchable
  • High frequency/Low size
  • Most annoying when done wrong

To Do, or not To Do

Don't: Lead with "Hey"

Do: Provide context

Don't: Wait to acknowledge

Do: Give a "Looking into it"

Don't: Ghost a conversation

Do: Let people know you're heading out/Update status

Tips and Tricks

@channel vs @here

  • @channel: Everyone joined to the channel
  • @here: Only those in the channel currently active
